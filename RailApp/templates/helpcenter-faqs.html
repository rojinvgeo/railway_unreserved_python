General Questions

Unreserved train tickets are tickets that allow you to travel on trains without a reserved seat. These tickets are typically available for shorter distances or on trains that do not offer reserved seating.

No, unreserved train tickets are non-transferable. The ticket is issued for a specific passenger and cannot be used by anyone else.

Unreserved train tickets do not guarantee a seat or a specific train. If you miss your intended train, you can use the same ticket for the next available train on the same route.

To book an unreserved train ticket, simply visit our website and follow the steps provided. Select your source and destination stations, choose the date and time of travel, and proceed to make the payment. Once your payment is successful, you can see your booking details in booking history section.

Payments Questions

To make a payment, select the desired unreserved ticket, proceed to the payment page

Online payments for unreserved tickets are generally non-refundable as per the railway rules. Therefore, it's advisable to review your booking details before making the payment.

Generally, there may be a nominal convenience fee or transaction charge applicable to online payments. Please refer to the website for specific details.
